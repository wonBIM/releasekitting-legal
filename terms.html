ReleaseKitting — Terms of Service

Last updated: 14 July 2026

1. What this is

ReleaseKitting is a personal, self-hosted tool that generates short-form promotional videos from an app's own screenshots and uploads them to the operator's own social media accounts (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram).

It is not offered to the public, has no other users, and is not a commercial service.

2. Who may use it

Only the operator (its author), on accounts they own and control. There is no sign-up and no third-party access.

3. What it does with connected accounts

When the operator connects a TikTok, YouTube or Instagram account, ReleaseKitting may:

  • Upload a video file the operator created, to that operator's own account.
  • Attach a title, description, caption and hashtags that the operator has reviewed and approved.

On TikTok specifically, an upload is delivered to the account's inbox as a draft. The operator completes or discards the post inside the TikTok app. ReleaseKitting does not publish public TikTok content.

It does not read, modify, or delete other people's content, and does not access data belonging to any other TikTok user.
